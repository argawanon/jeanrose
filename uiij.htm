30 Cute Cat Photos That May Make You a Cat Person

We don't blame you if you want to adopt one of these adorable breeds.

By Jessica Booth and Elizabeth BerryUpdated: Nov 07, 2022 7:00 PM EST
Use Arrow Keys to Navigate

Even if you aren't the biggest cat lover out there, you can still probably agree that kittens can get themselves into some pretty adorable positions — whether it's playing hide-and-seek in an old cardboard box or finding new napping locations that may or may not be slightly inconvenient for you, like on your laptop (though ideally it's just a sign your cat really loves you). Every cat owner has their favorite cute cat breed they may adopt over and over, but at the end of the day, all kittens and cats are adorable in their own way. These cute cat photos will brighten your day and maybe even convince you to browse pet adoption websites in search of a furry companion.

From mini kittens poking their heads into coffee mugs and tea cups to gorgeous ginger or black cats stretching after a nap, these images will capture your heart and make you laugh — especially if you are also a cat owner and have found your own pet in odd places more than a few times. You may even be inspired to try to replicate one or more of these photos with your own kitty. Any one of these cute cat photographs paired with a relatable cat quote will definitely rack up the likes on Instagram. Or, you can share your favorites with a fellow cat-loving friend or family member to instantly improve their mood.
